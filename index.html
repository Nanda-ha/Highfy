About Mudhra

Mudhra Dance Studio is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Indian classical dance forms. Our expert instructors bring centuries-old traditions to life through meticulous training and passionate teaching.


From Bharatanatyam to Kathak, Odissi to Kuchipudi, we offer comprehensive training for all levels – beginners to professional performers. Experience the divine art of mudhra (hand gestures) that tell timeless stories.

Our Classes

🌺 Bharatanatyam

The dance of grace and expression from Tamil Nadu. Master intricate footwork, expressive mudhras, and abhinaya.

🎭 Kathak

The storytelling art from North India. Learn mesmerizing spins, rhythmic footwork, and dramatic narratives.

🌸 Odissi

The poetry in motion from Odisha. Experience tribhanga posture and lyrical movements inspired by temple sculptures.

💃 Kuchipudi

The vibrant dance-drama from Andhra Pradesh. Combine dance, drama, and music in spectacular performances.
